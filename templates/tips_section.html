Health Tips

Evidence-based health tips to improve your daily wellness.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Carry a reusable bottle and set reminders to stay on track.

Hydration

Move Every Day

At least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily reduces risk of heart disease, diabetes, and depression.

Fitness

Eat the Rainbow

Include fruits and vegetables of different colors in your diet for a wide range of essential nutrients.

Nutrition

Prioritize Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep. Maintain a consistent schedule and limit screens before bed.

Sleep

Practice Mindfulness

Spend 10 minutes daily on meditation or deep breathing. It reduces stress, improves focus, and boosts emotional well-being.

Mental Health

Mindful Eating

Eat slowly, savor each bite, and stop when you're full. Avoid eating in front of screens for better digestion.
